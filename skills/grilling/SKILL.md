---
name: grilling
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design. Use when the user wants to stress-test a plan before building, or uses any 'grill' trigger phrases.
---

Interview me about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time, waiting for feedback on each question before continuing. Asking multiple questions at once is bewildering. Present the options with Ask Question tool.

Before asking questions, reflect and research yourself. Proactively explore the codebase, search for canonical practices and standard industry patterns on the Internet. Do not ask dumb questions which already have an obvious answer. The options you present to me must be viable, well-thought-out options that you have already carefully considered and fact-checked.
